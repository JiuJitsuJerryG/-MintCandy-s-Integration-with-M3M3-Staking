What Is M3M3, and Why Should $MintCandy Holders Care?

M3M3 is an innovative “stake-to-earn” mechanism designed to reward dedicated memecoin stakers. Every time someone swaps in or out of a memecoin, fees are generated. Instead of disappearing, these fees are redistributed to top stakers on M3M3. With $MintCandy now integrated, our community gains access to these rewards, incentivizing participation and long-term holding.

How to Earn Fee Rewards with $MintCandy on M3M3

  2. Once in the top 100, you begin accumulating fee rewards in real-time.
  3. The more you stake, the larger your share of the rewards.

The platform continuously tracks staking positions, and fee rewards are disbursed every few seconds. Increasing your stake boosts your rewards accordingly, fostering a fun and competitive environment.

Unique Parameters for $MintCandy Stakers

Origin of Fee Rewards: Permanently-Locked Liquidity

Every time a swap occurs in the $MintCandy liquidity pool, a portion of the transaction fee is earmarked as a reward. Since liquidity is permanently locked, these fees continue accumulating over time, providing a reliable revenue stream for M3M3 participants.

Continuous, Dynamic Fee Distribution

The 21-day rolling claimable reward period ensures smooth, continuous distribution. This aligns stakers’ interests with $MintCandy’s long-term performance, reducing volatility while maintaining fairness.

Compounding Your Earnings with $MintCandy
